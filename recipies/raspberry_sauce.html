Raspberry Sauce for Chicken

Chicken breasts have become such a mainstay of American Cuisine- the mere thought of them often bores me so much I could fall asleep. Imagine however the perfectly cooked chicken breast with a beautifully browned outer finish, a juicy and soft texture, and a wonderfully sweet and savory raspberry sauce to compliment their everending boredom.

This sauce recipe will take any perfectly cooked chicken to the next level of edible bliss

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Jar of Raspberry Allfruit (Paulana)
  • Equal part of Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 Tbs. Fresh Chopped Thyme
  • 1/4 tsp Kosher Salt

Steps

  • Combine raspberry and vinegar in the pan used to cook the chicken
  • Heat and stir until the two ingredients are combined and the all fruit is melted
  • Stir in Thyme and Kosher salt
  • Bring to a boil stiring freqently
  • Let the sauce happlily bubble until it just starts to thicken
  • Remove from burner and pour over the chicken
