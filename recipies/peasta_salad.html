Peasta Salad with Crab

Peasta Salad with Imitation Crabmeat

This is a great, easy, and tasty weeknight meal when the weather just starts to warm up!

The smokey flavor from the Glory black eye peas will compliment the savory cream sauce- plus it makes a ton so you can take it for lunch all week long.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Cup Good Seasons Italian Salad Dressing
  • 2 15oz Cans of Glory Foods Black-Eyed Peas
  • 16 oz Rotelle Pasta
  • 16 oz Imitation Crabmeat
  • One Broccoli Crown cut in bite size pieces
  • One Small Head of Cauliflower cut in bite size pieces
  • 3-4 Seeded Roma Tomatoes Chopped

Steps

  1. Cut up the Broccoli and Cauliflower and add to boiling water for 5 minutes
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions
  3. Clean and Chop tomatoes
  4. Drain and rinse blake-eyed peas
  5. After Broccoli and Cauliflower are done drain a shock with cold water and ice
  6. Drain and rinse the pasta in cold water
  7. Add pasta, black-eyed peas, broc, cauli, crabmeat, and tomatoes to a large salad bowl
  8. In a separate bowl wisk together mayo and salad dressing until smooth
  9. Gently fold in the dressing into the salad until it's well combined
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste
  11. Store left-overs in fridge- it's great for a quick lunch
